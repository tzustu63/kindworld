# Privacy Policy for KindWorld

**Last Updated: [Date]**

## Introduction

Welcome to KindWorld ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use the App.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information

We collect information that you provide directly to us when you:
- Create an account
- Complete your profile
- Participate in missions
- Redeem vouchers
- Contact customer support

**Personal information may include:**
- Name
- Email address
- Profile photo
- Biographical information
- Phone number (optional)
- Social media profile information (if you sign in via Google or Apple)

### Usage Information

We automatically collect certain information about your device and how you interact with the App:
- Device information (model, operating system, unique device identifiers)
- Log information (IP address, access times, app features used)
- Location information (with your permission)
- Mission participation data
- Points transactions
- Voucher redemptions
- App usage analytics

### Information from Third Parties

We may receive information about you from third-party services:
- **Google OAuth**: Profile information (name, email, profile picture)
- **Apple Sign-In**: Email address, name (or private relay email)
- **Firebase Services**: Authentication and analytics data

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

### To Provide and Maintain Our Service
- Create and manage your account
- Process mission participation
- Award and track Compassion Points
- Process voucher redemptions
- Display leaderboards and rankings
- Award badges and achievements

### To Improve Our Service
- Analyze usage patterns
- Develop new features
- Optimize app performance
- Conduct research and analytics

### To Communicate With You
- Send transactional emails (account verification, password reset)
- Provide customer support
- Send notifications about missions and rewards
- Share important updates about the App

### To Ensure Security
- Detect and prevent fraud
- Monitor for suspicious activity
- Enforce our Terms of Service
- Protect user safety

### For Marketing (With Your Consent)
- Send promotional communications
- Personalize your experience
- Show relevant mission recommendations

## How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

### With Your Consent
We may share your information when you give us explicit permission to do so.

### With Service Providers
We share information with third-party vendors who perform services on our behalf:
- **Firebase (Google)**: Cloud hosting, authentication, analytics
- **Cloud storage providers**: For storing profile photos and mission images
- **Payment processors**: For processing voucher redemptions (if applicable)

### For Legal Reasons
We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to:
- Legal processes (subpoenas, court orders)
- Government requests
- Protection of our rights and property
- Investigation of potential violations
- Protection of user safety

### Business Transfers
If KindWorld is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

### Public Information
Certain information is publicly visible by design:
- Your display name
- Profile photo
- Leaderboard ranking
- Earned badges
- Volunteer hours (if you choose to display them)

## Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to:
- Provide our services
- Comply with legal obligations
- Resolve disputes
- Enforce our agreements

When you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal information within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal purposes.

## Your Rights and Choices

### Account Information
You can update your account information at any time through the App's profile settings.

### Location Information
You can control location permissions through your device settings. Note that disabling location may limit certain features like location-based mission discovery.

### Marketing Communications
You can opt out of promotional emails by:
- Clicking the "unsubscribe" link in emails
- Adjusting notification settings in the App
- Contacting us at privacy@kindworld.app

### Data Access and Portability
You have the right to:
- Access your personal information
- Request a copy of your data
- Correct inaccurate information
- Delete your account and data

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@kindworld.app.

### Do Not Track
We do not currently respond to "Do Not Track" signals from web browsers.

## Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information, including:
- Encryption of data in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
- Encryption of data at rest
- Regular security assessments
- Access controls and authentication
- Secure cloud infrastructure (Firebase)

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## Children's Privacy

KindWorld is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at privacy@kindworld.app, and we will delete such information.

## International Data Transfers

We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information in accordance with this Privacy Policy and applicable laws.

## California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):
- Right to know what personal information is collected
- Right to know if personal information is sold or disclosed
- Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information
- Right to deletion of personal information
- Right to non-discrimination for exercising your rights

We do not sell personal information. To exercise your CCPA rights, contact us at privacy@kindworld.app.

## European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):
- Right to access your personal data
- Right to rectification of inaccurate data
- Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
- Right to restrict processing
- Right to data portability
- Right to object to processing
- Right to withdraw consent

To exercise your GDPR rights, contact us at privacy@kindworld.app.

- Our legitimate interests
